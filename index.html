About Lucas

Working-class representation for Ward 1

Lucas is running against an establishment incumbent who raised over $40,000 in her last campaign — and still has no tangible policy proposals on her website. This campaign is different. It is funded by neighbors, run by community, and accountable only to the people of Ward 1.
