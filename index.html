About the candidate

Who is Lucas?

Lucas King-Cochran is a student and service worker who lives the cost-of-living crisis Ann Arbor families face every day. He is running as an independent democratic socialist because he believes working-class voices — not corporate donors — should shape our city.

His opponent is an establishment incumbent who raised over $40,000 in her last campaign and has no tangible policy proposals. Lucas's campaign runs on people power.
